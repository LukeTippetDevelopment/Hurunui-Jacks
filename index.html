Whether you’re on a long New Zealand journey or a short weekend getaway, Hurunui Jacks is the perfect place to take a break along the West Coast Wilderness trail. Set on 15 acres of private, native foreste land, with its own lake, and the Kaniere River on your doorstep, you’ll love the peace and tranquility that Hurunui Jacks has to offer.
